Make School Holidays Smart & Fun

STEM holiday camps in Melbourne designed for curious young minds who love technology, creativity and innovation.

Our STEM holiday camps in Melbourne are created for kids who want to explore coding, robotics and engineering in a fun, structured way. Instead of just screen time, your child builds real projects they can be proud of.

What Happens at Camp?

Each day combines learning, hands-on building, challenge activities and a showcase session.

During our school holiday STEM camps, students:

  • Build and program robots
  • Create coding projects and simple games
  • Design mini engineering challenges
  • Learn teamwork and problem solving
  • Present their projects confidently

Each day is structured with:

  • Learning session for new STEM concepts
  • Hands-on build time for coding and robotics projects
  • Challenge activities to apply skills creatively
  • Showcase session where students share what they built

What Makes Our Holiday Camps Different?

A balance of fun, practical learning and future-ready STEM skills.

  • ✔ Practical, hands-on STEM learning
  • ✔ Small groups for personal attention
  • ✔ Industry-aligned coding and robotics skills
  • ✔ Supportive, experienced instructors
  • ✔ Fun experiences that build future-ready skills

We teach skills that matter in tomorrow’s world, from logical thinking and creativity to collaboration and communication.
