At The Coders Studio, our robotics classes in Melbourne help children turn ideas into real machines. Students don’t just watch — they design, build and program robots from scratch using age-appropriate tools.

We combine coding fundamentals, electronics and sensors, engineering thinking and real-world problem solving to create a learning environment where future engineers can experiment, fail safely and innovate.

Our programs are designed for students aged 7–16 who are curious about how technology and robotics work in the real world.

A structured robotics curriculum that grows with your child – from block-based coding to Python and real engineering concepts.

Our robotics program in Melbourne includes:

  • Basic electronics for safe, practical circuits
  • Block-based coding for beginners to understand logic
  • Python and advanced coding for older students
  • Real-world robotics challenges and problem-solving tasks

Students build projects such as:

  • Automated smart devices and home-style gadgets
  • Mini engineering prototypes that simulate real products
